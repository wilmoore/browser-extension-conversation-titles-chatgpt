# Privacy Policy for Conversation Titles for ChatGPT

**Last Updated:** December 2024

**Publisher:** SavvyAI
**Contact:** support@savvyai.dev

---

## Overview

Conversation Titles for ChatGPT is a browser extension that displays your ChatGPT conversation titles for easy reference and copying. This privacy policy explains how the extension handles your data.

---

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any data.**

This extension:
- Does **not** collect personal information
- Does **not** collect browsing history
- Does **not** track your usage
- Does **not** send any data to external servers
- Does **not** use analytics or tracking tools

---

## Data Storage

The extension stores only your preferences locally on your device:

| Data | Purpose | Location |
|------|---------|----------|
| Copy format preferences | Remember your preferred copy shortcuts | Chrome local storage |
| Audio feedback setting | Remember your sound preference | Chrome local storage |

This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not synced across devices
- Can be deleted by removing the extension

---

## Permissions Explained

### Clipboard Write
**Why:** To copy conversation titles to your clipboard when you click them.
**Scope:** Only writes to clipboard when you explicitly click the title.

### Storage
**Why:** To save your preferences (copy formats, audio feedback toggle).
**Scope:** Stores only on your local device.

### Host Permissions (chatgpt.com, chat.openai.com)
**Why:** The extension only works on ChatGPT pages where conversation titles exist.
**Scope:** Cannot access any other websites.

---

## Third-Party Services

This extension does **not**:
- Connect to third-party servers
- Use third-party analytics
- Include third-party tracking
- Make any network requests

All functionality runs entirely within your browser.

---

## Data Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted:
- There is no data to secure on our end
- Your preferences are protected by Chrome's built-in extension storage security
- We have no access to your ChatGPT conversations

---

## Children's Privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.
