# AI Screenshot Generation Prompts

Use these prompts with AI image generators (Ideogram, Midjourney, DALL-E, etc.) to create compelling Chrome Web Store screenshots.

## Context

**Extension**: Conversation Titles for ChatGPT
**Value Prop**: Always see and copy your ChatGPT conversation title
**Key Features**:
- Always-visible title in footer
- Click-to-copy with multiple formats
- Configurable shortcuts (Ctrl+click, Shift+click, etc.)

## CWS Screenshot Requirements

- **Dimensions**: 1280x800 or 640x400 (16:10 aspect ratio)
- **Count**: Up to 5 screenshots
- **Format**: PNG or JPEG

---

## Prompt 1: Hero Screenshot (Main Value Prop)

> A clean, modern browser screenshot mockup showing ChatGPT interface. At the bottom of the chat area, there's a subtle but visible conversation title "Planning Q4 Marketing Strategy" displayed in a sleek footer bar. A cursor hovers over the title with a tooltip showing "Click to copy". Minimal, professional design with soft shadows. Light mode interface. 16:10 aspect ratio.

**Focus**: Show the title is always visible, clickable

## Prompt 2: Copy Formats Feature

> An infographic-style image showing 4 different clipboard outputs from clicking a conversation title. Each shows a different format: (1) Just title "My Conversation", (2) Title + URL as markdown link, (3) Title with project prefix "[Project] Title", (4) Full context with all metadata. Clean icons representing each format. Modern flat design, blue accent colors. 16:10 aspect ratio.

**Focus**: Show the flexibility of copy formats

**Focus**: Show real-world use case

## Style Notes

- **Avoid**: Cluttered designs, too much text, low contrast
- **Prefer**: Clean whitespace, clear visual hierarchy, professional look
- **Colors**: Match ChatGPT's green/dark theme or use neutral modern palette
- **Typography**: Sans-serif, readable at thumbnail size
